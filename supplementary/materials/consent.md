# Consent form

This is the consent text participants read at the beginning of session 1. A parallel consent form with matching content was presented at the screening stage prior to eligibility assessment.

---

Dear Participants,

Welcome to our study on the impact of imaginal interventions via writing on anxiety. We appreciate your collaboration in this important research endeavor.

The study you are about to participate in consists of five parts:

- **Part 1 — 80 minutes, £12:** completion of questionnaires + writing task.
- **Part 2 — 30 minutes, £4.50:** writing task (to be completed 1–2 days after the first part).
- **Part 3 — 30 minutes, £4.50:** writing task (to be completed 1–2 days after the second part).
- **Part 4 — 60 minutes, £9:** completion of questionnaires + writing task (to be completed 1–2 days after the third part).
- **Part 5 — 10 minutes, £1.50:** completion of short questionnaires (to be completed 1 week after the fourth part).

**BONUS** payment for completion of ALL PARTS: £5.

The TOTAL payment that can be achieved for participating in the multi-part study is £36.50.

You are currently in the first part.

During this first session, you will complete questionnaires to provide us with a baseline understanding of your anxiety. These questionnaires are essential for assessing the effectiveness of the intervention and understanding how it works. Afterward, you will participate in a prolonged writing intervention with imaginal activation, designed to reduce anxiety levels and improve overall mental health.

We want to make it clear that participation in this study is completely voluntary. You have the option to skip any questions or parts of the questionnaire that you find irrelevant by indicating *NR* (not relevant). In the event that you decide to withdraw from the study, you will be paid in proportion to the parts of the questionnaire that you completed. Please be aware that any invalid responses (such as typing non-content text in text boxes) may disqualify you from receiving payment for participating in the experiment.

If you have any questions, concerns, or problems during the study, please contact us using the internal messaging system of Prolific. We want to ensure a positive and safe experience for all participants. If you feel you need mental health assistance, visit [findahelpline.com](https://findahelpline.com) for resources and support.

We greatly appreciate your collaboration and consider you valuable collaborators, not merely participants. This session should take approximately 80 minutes, and you will be compensated £12. We thank you for your participation in this journey with us.

We take security, privacy, and ethics very seriously.

Sincerely,

Elad Zlotnick

---

**Declaration.** Participants were asked to indicate whether they agreed to participate:

- *I agree*
- *I do not agree*

Participants who selected *I do not agree* were redirected to exit the study without completing any further items. The screening stage also terminated if participants declined consent.
